Just Weave It

2019

In collaboration with Yap Zi Ning

After understanding the basics of culling, we decided to explore how to create weave patterns using grasshopper. We first focused on weaves on a two-dimensional scale, creating a unit of a chosen pattern before scaling it. This method also applies for weaves that involve more than two dimensions, however, the units have to be handled as parallelograms instead of rectangles. You can find out more here.

Although we managed to produce the patterns we set out to make, we are still working on how to avoid intersections after piping the curves and connect the scaled up units seamlessly.
